Email rendering services generated.

Manual steps:

1. In ApplicationDependencyInjectionExtension:
   services.AddScoped<IEmailTemplateRendererService, EmailTemplateRendererService>();
   services.AddScoped<IEmailNotificationService, EmailNotificationService>();

2. In CreateRegistrationHandler constructor, add:
   IEmailNotificationService emailNotificationService

3. Inside constructor:
   _emailNotificationService = emailNotificationService;

4. After registration has been saved and has Id:
   await _emailNotificationService.CreateRegistrationEmailLogAsync(
       EmailTemplateKey.RegistrationCreated,
       registration,
       user,
       edition,
       passType,
       cancellationToken);

5. For admin-triggered emails later, reuse:
   CreateRegistrationEmailLogAsync(EmailTemplateKey.WaitingPartner, ...)
   CreateRegistrationEmailLogAsync(EmailTemplateKey.PaymentConfirmed, ...)
   CreateRegistrationEmailLogAsync(EmailTemplateKey.PaymentFailed, ...)
   CreateRegistrationEmailLogAsync(EmailTemplateKey.RegistrationCancelled, ...)
